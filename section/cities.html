Middle Earth

Minas Tirith

Minas Tirith, the Tower of Guard, was the capital city of Gondor.

Edoras

Edoras was the capital of Rohan, it was also where Rohan's kings were buried.

Rivendell

Rivendell, also known as Imladris in Sindarin, was an Elven town and the house of Elrond located in Middle-earth.

Lothlorien

Lothlórien, also known as Lórien, was the vast woodland realm of the Galadhrim elves. Home of Lady Galadriel and Lord Celeborn of Doriath.

Mordor

Mordor, Sauron's realm, was a black, volcanic plain in the southeast of Middle-earth.

Isengard

Isengard, also known as Angrenost, was Saruman's domain, and the home to the tower of Orthanc.

Erebor

The Lonely Mountain, known in Sindarin as Erebor,was the source of the river Running and a major Dwarven stronghold, the Kingdom under the Mountain. In the latter half of the Third Age, it became the greatest Dwarf city in Middle-earth.

The Shire

The Shire was a realm in northwestern Middle-earth, inhabited by hobbits. It was located in the region of Eriador.
