Middle Earth

Aragorn

Son of Arathorn. Heir to the throne of Gondor. One of the remaining Dunédain

Gandalf

One of the Istari, a wizard, the wisest of the Maiar, also known as Olórin and Mithrandir

Frodo

A hobbit of the Shire. The bearer of The One Ring

Legolas

Son of Thranduil. Prince of the elven Woodland Realm of Mirkwood

Gimli

Son of Glóin. Dwarf of the House of Durin

Galadriel

Lady of the Woods of Lothlórien. She bore Nenya, ring of water, one of the three Elven rings

Elrond

Lord of Rivendell. Half-elf, father of Arwen. Bearer of Vilya, the ring of air

Bilbo

A hobbit of the Shire. The previous bearer of The One Ring. Frodo Baggins's uncle

Saruman

The White Wizard, First of the Istaria, ruler of Isengard, also known as Curumo and Curunír

Merry & Pippin

Hobbits of the Shire. Members of the Fellowship of the Ring, friends of Frodo and Sam

Sam

Hobbit of the Shire. Frodo's closest and most dependable companion, the most loyal of the Fellowship
